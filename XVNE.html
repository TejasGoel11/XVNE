Born at the crossroads of genres, XVNE is an artist who refuses to be defined by any single sound. From the hypnotic rhythms of afrobeats to the raw energy of hip-hop, from the emotional depths of R&B to the pulsating highs of electronic music — every track is a new world.

With 50M+ streams and performances across three continents, the journey has been relentless. Music is not a genre — it's a language spoken across every border.
